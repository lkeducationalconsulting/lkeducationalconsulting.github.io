College visits!

One of the top priorities for any college advisor is to tour colleges and universities. Although Laurie Kiguchi is based in Santa Cruz, California, she regularly travels around the country and beyond to visit schools and speak with admissions directors, university presidents and provosts, faculty, and current students.

Laurie visits dozens of schools each year, sometimes in organized counselor-oriented tours and sometimes on her own to meet privately with admissions representatives. Visiting nearby colleges is always a priority whenever she travels.

Visiting colleges and universities is invaluable for prospective students and families as well. Seeing, hearing, feeling what a school is like is something you can't get from a website. Laurie provides guidance on schools to visit, what to look for once there, and how to follow up.
