Contact Laurie!


Laurie Kiguchi is a college and career advisor at LK Educational Consulting in Santa Cruz, California. She is happy to meet with you to explore how she can help with your college or career planning needs.

Laurie offers a complimentary 30-minute introductory session to explain her approach to the college or career planning process and give everyone a chance to get to know one another. This can be extended to a working session where she can review a student's individual record and interests, or it can be limited to the initial 30 minutes. Laurie loves meeting new people and working with them toward achieving their goals.
