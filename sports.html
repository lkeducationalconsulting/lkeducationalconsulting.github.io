Playing sports in college

Here are links to relevant websites for the major varsity college sports. The majority are varsity sports organized through the NCAA and NAIA. Some sports are governed by organizations specific to the sport. Some sports are a combination.

If you are serious about playing varsity sports in college, be sure to discuss this with Laurie!
